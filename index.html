Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2023

Thank you for choosing Time Balance. We prioritize your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our data collection, use, and sharing practices. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data while you use Time Balance.

Our commitment to privacy

We do not collect any information that can personally identify our users. Instead, we only collect anonymous data, which helps us address technical issues, track purchases, and determine how to improve the app for everyone.

Data Collection and Use

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymized data, such as crash reports, device types, and aggregated statistics related to the core features of the app. This data helps us identify and fix bugs, as well as enhance app features. You can find more information about both services here:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

RevenueCat is used for anonymous purchase tracking. When a purchase occurs, we only have access to the purchase details, such as the product that was purchased, the timestamp and the country; no other data is collected, ensuring that purchase information cannot be linked to a user's identity. More information about RevenueCat is available here:
https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/

All your personal data, including projects, tasks, and tags, are stored locally on your device, ensuring that we cannot access this data.

Sharing information and user responsibility

Time Balance lets you export and share your data, such as projects, time tracking sessions and tasks. This is helpful for making backups or moving to a new device. However, we are not responsible for the protection of any data that you choose to share or export from the app. It is your responsibility to ensure the protection of such data, and we encourage you to only share your data through channels you trust.

Your consent

By using Time Balance, you agree to this privacy policy.
